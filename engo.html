About Us

We Build Families,
Not Just Shelters

Founded in 1995, Little Hearts Orphanage has been a beacon of hope for hundreds of children. Our mission is simple yet profound: to provide every child with the love, care, and opportunities they deserve.

We believe that every child has unlimited potential. With proper nurturing, education, and support, they can grow into confident individuals ready to make their mark on the world.
